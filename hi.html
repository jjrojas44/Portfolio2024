Jurassic Park Poster This graphic design embodies the ultimate Jurassic showdown, splitting the legacy of *Jurassic Park* and *Jurassic World* into two distinct yet seamlessly blended halves. The top half represents *Jurassic Park*, set against a fiery red-orange backdrop with a rugged stone grunge texture, evoking the raw, untamed essence of the original. The iconic *Jurassic Park* logo stands bold, accompanied by the legendary T. rex, Rexy, reigning over the chaos. In contrast, the bottom half showcases the sleek, modern aesthetic of *Jurassic World*, with a cool-toned blue background infused with metallic textures, reflecting innovation and control. The *Jurassic World* logo gleams amidst shades of icy blue, grey, and white, while Blue, the intelligent Velociraptor, stands as its fierce representative. The two worlds collide in a battle of past versus present—*which side will you choose?*

Case Study:

My disney experience app redesign
Project Overview
  • Project Name: My disney experience app Redesign
  • Role: UX/UI Designer (Solo Designer)
  • Tools Used: Figma, Figjam, Photoshop, Illustrator, Google Drive/Docs, Coolors
  • Timeline: 2 Weeks
  • Objective: Redesign the My Disney Experience app to enhance UX with improved navigation, accessibility, and a modernized UI while optimizing performance for faster access to key features.
Value Added

The redesigned My Disney Experience app provides users with a more intuitive interface, faster navigation, and improved accessibility.

Problem Statement

Challenges with the Existing App:

  • Complex Navigation: Users struggle to quickly access key features like park reservations, wait times, and ticketing.
  • Cumbersome Booking & Planning: The reservation system is unintuitive, making it difficult to manage Lightning Lane selections and dining plans.
  • Outdated UI & Inconsistent Branding: The visual design lacks modern Disney magic and does not fully align with the brand's immersive storytelling.
  • Limited Personalization & Interactivity: The app provides generic recommendations instead of personalized experiences based on user preferences.

Why a Redesign?

  • To create a more user-friendly, visually engaging, and efficient app experience.
  • To streamline the booking process, improving access to park planning tools.
  • To introduce immersive features like AR navigation, real-time updates, and gamified rewards that enhance guest engagement.
  • To ensure the app reflects Disney’s innovation and storytelling, making every digital interaction as magical as the park experience.
