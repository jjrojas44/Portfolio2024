Jurassic Park Poster This graphic design embodies the ultimate Jurassic showdown, splitting the legacy of *Jurassic Park* and *Jurassic World* into two distinct yet seamlessly blended halves. The top half represents *Jurassic Park*, set against a fiery red-orange backdrop with a rugged stone grunge texture, evoking the raw, untamed essence of the original. The iconic *Jurassic Park* logo stands bold, accompanied by the legendary T. rex, Rexy, reigning over the chaos. In contrast, the bottom half showcases the sleek, modern aesthetic of *Jurassic World*, with a cool-toned blue background infused with metallic textures, reflecting innovation and control. The *Jurassic World* logo gleams amidst shades of icy blue, grey, and white, while Blue, the intelligent Velociraptor, stands as its fierce representative.
