Jurassic park Poster

This graphic design captures the thrilling essence of the original Jurassic Park movie, featuring the characters Tim and Lex Murphy hiding in a foggy jungle teeming with dinosaurs. Utilizing dramatic lighting and deep shadows, the poster creates a sense of suspense and wonder, immersing viewers in a world where nature and prehistoric creatures coexist. The combination of eerie fog and vibrant colors enhances the mystical atmosphere, making it a striking homage to the beloved franchise.
