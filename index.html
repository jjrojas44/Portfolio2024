I'm

UI Designer, UX Developer, Graphic Designer, Front-End Developer

My name is Juan Jose Rojas. I’m a UX/UI designer, originally from Medellin , Colombia. I now live in sunnyside Florida in Orlando.

I’m an aspiring UX/UI visual designer with a background focused on digital design, coding, and front-end software development. Growing up, I've always been told that I have an artistic and creative mind. Combining that with my love of design and everything involving front-end software development, I feel like I have found my perfect role. I believe you can make innovative, colorful, and beautiful designs that still hold the quorum of style, professionalism, and consistency. A creative mind always makes the work bright, and I aim to push products to new heights through designs that are backed by data, research, rapid iteration, and visually beautiful, user-focused UI.
