I’m Juan Jose Rojas, a UX/UI and visual designer focused on building clean, user-centered digital experiences, from product UI to game and interactive interfaces.

I am a UX/UI and visual designer with a foundation in front-end development, focused on creating usable, scalable digital experiences across web, mobile, and interactive platforms. My work centers on improving clarity, accessibility, and efficiency through thoughtful interface design and consistent visual systems.

I bring experience in product design, prototyping, and user-centered problem solving, translating requirements into structured, intuitive interfaces that support both user goals and business objectives. I am particularly interested in contributing to teams developing digital products, game interfaces, or emerging interactive technologies where design decisions directly impact engagement, usability, and performance.
