U-Game-U-PLAY

An all You need Video game information website .

×

Case Study:

A True Gamers Source of Video Game Information
Project Overview
  • Project Name: Universal Studios Website Redesign
  • Role: UX/UI Designer (Solo Designer)
  • Tools Used: Figma, Figjam, Adobe XD, Photoshop, Google Drive/Docs, Coolors
  • Timeline: 4 Weeks
  • Objective: To provide a seamless gaming hub with news, reviews, deals, and a vibrant community, enhancing the player experience.
Value Added

A value added to the case study for "u-game-u-play" could be to implement a data-driven approach to content creation and user engagement. This could involve tracking user behavior and interests to understand which topics and games are most popular among the website's visitors. The website could also collect user reviews and feedback to refine further and personalize its content. Additionally, analytics and A/B testing could be leveraged to optimize the user experience and increase user engagement. Moreover, incorporating a reward system for active participation on the forum or submitting reviews can increase engagement and promote sharing of valuable and exciting content. By taking a data-driven approach and prioritizing personalization and user engagement, "u-game-u-play" can differentiate itself from other video game information websites and become a valuable resource for gamers and enthusiasts alike.

Problem Statement

Challenges with the Existing Platform:

  • Scattered Gaming Content: Users struggle to find relevant news, reviews, and deals efficiently.
  • Poor Community Engagement: Limited interaction tools make discussions and forums less engaging.
  • Unintuitive Navigation: The layout lacks clear pathways, making discovery difficult.
  • Inconsistent Visual Identity: The design fails to create a cohesive and immersive gaming experience.

Why a Redesign?

  • To centralize gaming content, ensuring users can easily access news, reviews, and deals in one place.
  • To enhance community engagement with interactive features and a seamless forum experience.
  • To streamline navigation for better user flow and accessibility.
  • To create a visually dynamic and engaging platform that resonates with gamers.
Research & User Insights
Persona
User Insight

Steven, a 23-year-old front-end software developer from Florida, is strongly inclined toward the latest and most advanced video games and consoles. His desire to remain up-to-date on the latest releases and deals is driven by a keen awareness of the inadequacies of existing information sources in highlighting obscure games and unnoticed deals. As a result, Steven is actively seeking a reliable and comprehensive resource that can cater to his needs and provide him with the latest news and updates on video game releases and deals.

How Might We
  • Centralize gaming content to ensure users can effortlessly access news, reviews, and deals in one place.
  • Enhance community engagement by integrating interactive discussion forums and user-driven content.
  • Improve site navigation to create a seamless, intuitive browsing experience for all users.
  • Develop a visually engaging platform that reflects the excitement and diversity of the gaming world.
