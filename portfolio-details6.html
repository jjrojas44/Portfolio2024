U-Game-U-PLAY

An all You need Video game information website .

Case Study:

A True Gamers Source of Video Game Information
Project Overview
  • Project Name: Universal Studios Website Redesign
  • Role: UX/UI Designer (Solo Designer)
  • Tools Used: Figma, Adobe XD, Photoshop, Google Drive/Docs, Coolors
  • Timeline: 5 Weeks
  • Objective: Enhance usability, navigation, and booking experience while modernizing the website's design for a seamless user journey.
Value Added

The "u-game-u-play" case study receives value enhancement through data-driven methods which improve content creation and user engagement. The site can improve its offerings by tracking user behavior while collecting feedback about popular topics. The platform should implement A/B testing and reward users who participate in reviews or forum activities to boost engagement. The strategy would make "u-game-u-play" the primary destination for gamers.

Problem Statement

Challenges with the Existing Competition:

  • Scattered Gaming Content: Users often struggle to locate relevant news, reliable reviews, and the best gaming deals efficiently within one platform.
  • Poor Community Engagement: Limited interaction tools and uninspired forums reduce opportunities for meaningful discussions and user retention.
  • Unintuitive Navigation: Disorganized layouts and unclear site pathways hinder seamless content discovery and ease of use.
  • Inconsistent Visual Identity: The design lacks a cohesive, immersive feel that aligns with gamer expectations for a dynamic, modern platform.

Why a Redesign?

  • To centralize gaming content into one intuitive hub where users can easily access breaking news, detailed reviews, and real-time deal alerts without needing multiple sites.
  • To build a more interactive, user-driven community with upgraded forums, discussion threads, and social features to boost engagement and brand loyalty.
  • To streamline the platformâ€™s navigation structure, improving usability, reducing bounce rates, and supporting a faster content discovery experience.
  • To deliver a visually compelling, dynamic, and modern UI style that captures the energy and diversity of gaming culture while elevating brand identity.

Research

Persona

User Insight

Steven is a 23-year-old front-end software developer from Florida with a passion for staying ahead of the curve in gaming. He values up-to-the-minute releases, hidden deals, and reliable game reviews but finds existing platforms scattered, inconsistent, and lacking efficiency. Steven seeks a centralized, trustworthy, and detailed platform to keep him informed and connected with like-minded gamers.

How Might We

  • Centralize gaming content into one streamlined platform that delivers real-time news, reviews, and deal alerts efficiently.
  • Enhance community engagement through revitalized forums, user-generated content hubs, and interactive discussion spaces.
  • Improve site navigation with a simplified, consistent, and intuitive content structure for faster browsing and discovery.
  • Create a visually dynamic platform that captures the excitement of gaming culture while maintaining ease of use and accessibility.

POV Statement

Steven Young, a passionate gamer and software developer, requires a clean, accessible gaming platform because he wants immediate, reliable updates on games, reviews, and deals without the hassle of scouring multiple fragmented sites. By creating a modern, community-driven hub with real-time updates and social tools, we can help Steven stay informed, entertained, and connected with his gaming peers.

Problem Statement

The existing u-Game-U-Play platform fails to meet user expectations, lacking real-time content updates and modern features, leading to user dissatisfaction and retention issues. Gamers demand immediate, engaging information. By adding real-time news feeds, deal alerts, and interactive features, we can dramatically improve user retention and establish the platform as a leader in gaming news and community experiences.
