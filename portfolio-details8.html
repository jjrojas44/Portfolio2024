Metalla Pretiosa

Metalla Pretiosa is an online platform for buying, selling, trading, and blogging about precious metals, offering a hub for enthusiasts and traders alike.

Case Study:

Get all the precious metals your heart desires.
Brief

Metalla Pretiosa is an online platform for buying, selling, trading, precious metals.

Project

For Profit Redesign

Role

Solo Design

Time

7 Week Project

Value Added

Designing Metalla Pretiosa on Figma added significant value by streamlining navigation, boosting user engagement, and ensuring accessibility. The redesign enhanced user trust through a professional aesthetic, improved conversion rates, and provided a seamless experience across all devices.

Persona
User Insight

Rodrigo Bordega, a dynamic stock trader with a B.A. in Business Administration and Finance, seeks a seamless trading experience that combines cutting-edge financial tools with real-time market insights. As an outgoing and ambitious professional, Rodrigo values platforms that not only cater to his need for precise market data but also foster a sense of community engagement. His frustration with market volatility and regulatory changes drives a deep desire for a user-friendly platform that provides stability, adaptability, and a supportive environment for continuous learning. Rodrigo's journey reflects a quest for a balanced and fulfilling trading lifestyle that seamlessly integrates professional success, personal well-being, and impactful community involvement.

How Might We

How might we design a user-centric website that offers a seamless and secure platform for individuals like Rodrigo Bordega to efficiently buy, sell, and trade gold, silver, platinum, and other precious metals? How might we integrate real-time market data and expert insights to empower users with informed decision-making? Additionally, how might we address concerns related to the transparency and authenticity of precious metal transactions, ensuring a trustworthy environment? In creating this platform, how might we prioritize user-friendly interfaces, enhance accessibility, and provide a comprehensive educational resource to cater to users with varying levels of expertise in the precious metals market

POV Statement

As an ambitious stock trader and investor like Rodrigo Bordega, navigating the complexities of the precious metals market is hindered by the absence of a dedicated online platform that seamlessly integrates buying, selling, and trading of gold, silver, platinum, and other precious metals. Unlike current options, Rodrigo requires a trustworthy and transparent platform that not only provides real-time market insights but also fosters a sense of community engagement. He desires a user-friendly website that addresses concerns related to transaction authenticity and market volatility, offering a holistic experience that aligns with his multifaceted goals of financial success, continuous learning, and contributing to the community.

Problem Statement

The current landscape lacks a comprehensive and user-centric online platform tailored for individuals like Rodrigo Bordega, a dynamic stock trader seeking efficient solutions for buying, selling, and trading precious metals such as gold, silver, and platinum. Existing platforms fall short in providing a seamless integration of these functionalities, leaving users without a transparent and trustworthy space for their transactions. The absence of real-time market insights, coupled with concerns about transaction authenticity and market volatility, contributes to a significant gap in meeting the diverse needs of individuals navigating the precious metals market. Addressing these challenges presents an opportunity to develop a specialized platform that not only caters to Rodrigo's ambitions for financial success but also aligns with his desire for continuous learning and community engagement in the precious metals industry.
